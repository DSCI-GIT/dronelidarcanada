Why this service

For sites where coverage, terrain, and turnaround matter.

Large-area topographic work is where drone LiDAR can create the most practical value. Aerial collection covers substantial ground efficiently, while LiDAR processing turns raw returns into usable terrain data.

This is especially useful for development land, rural and vegetated parcels, access-limited areas, drainage corridors, infrastructure routes, and pre-design investigations.

  • Survey-grade workflows with ground control and checkpoints where required
  • Terrain products scoped to engineering and planning needs
  • Data deliverables prepared for CAD, GIS, and project review workflows
Common uses

Topo survey outputs for real project decisions.

Pre-design and site planning
