Vegetation advantage

When vegetation hides the ground, LiDAR earns its place.

Photogrammetry works well when the surface is visible. On vegetated land, it often models the top of the cover instead of the ground underneath.

LiDAR captures returns through gaps in the vegetation, giving the processing team a stronger basis for bare-earth terrain, grade, drainage, access, corridors, and site constraints.
