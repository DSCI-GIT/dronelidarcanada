Vegetation advantage

When trees hide the ground, LiDAR is the right conversation.

Photogrammetry is great when the surface is visible. On vegetated land, it often maps the top of the cover instead of the terrain underneath.

LiDAR gives us a better path to bare-earth data, which helps teams look at grade, drainage, access, corridors, and site constraints with less guesswork.
