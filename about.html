Drone Services Canada Inc. is based in Southern Ontario and works on professional drone data projects across Canada. LiDAR is a core service, especially for large-area topo and bare-earth terrain work.

The goal here is simple: show when drone LiDAR makes sense, what kind of files you can ask for, and how to start a quote conversation with the main company.

Positioning

Focused on professional LiDAR work.

This is not the lane for hobby drone work, small residential lots, or one-off media requests. It is for teams that need measurable terrain data and usable deliverables.

Large-area topo

Efficient aerial collection and processing for substantial sites and terrain-driven decisions.

Bare-earth terrain

LiDAR classification workflows for sites where vegetation hides the ground.
